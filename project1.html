END-TO-END BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE PROJECT

Global Electronics Retail Analysis

An end-to-end business intelligence project analyzing global electronics retail data to evaluate overall business performance, product performance, customer segments, and store performance using PostgreSQL and Power BI.

Explore Project

Business Problem

We are dealing with a dataset of global electronics retailer company which operates across multiple countries, stores, and product categories, which has around 60,000 sales transactions over time. Currently, the company lacks a centralized view of its business performance, making it difficult to monitor key metrics, identify high-performing products and regions, and understand customer purchasing patterns.

Project Objectives

  • Analyze sales performance across products, categories, brands, stores, and countries.
  • Identify top-performing products, regions, and customer segments.
  • Generate actionable insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven business decisions.

Data Cleaning

Before analysis, the dataset was assessed and cleaned to improve data quality and ensure reliable reporting.

The data cleaning process included:

  • Renamed columns for consistency and readability.
  • Checked for missing values across all tables.
  • Verified duplicate records and primary key uniqueness.
  • Standardized inconsistent date formats.
  • Converted wrong data types to accurate ones.
  • Created cleaned SQL views for further analysis.

SQL Analysis

The analysis focused on 11 business questions:

  1. What is the overall performance of the business?
  2. What is the yearly revenue and profit trend?
  3. Which brands generate the highest revenue and profit?
  4. Which product categories generate the highest revenue and profit?
  5. Which product sub categories generate the highest revenue and profit?
  6. Which individual products generate the highest revenue and profit?
  7. Which product has the highest revenue within each subcategory?
  8. Which countries contribute the highest revenue and profit?
  9. Which states generate the highest revenue and profit?
  10. Which customer age groups contribute the most revenue and profit?
  11. How do physical and online stores compare in revenue and profit?

The resulting insights were then visualized in an interactive Power BI dashboard to support business decision-making.

Insights

Overall Performance

  • The retailer generated $55.8M in total revenue and $32.7M in total profit, with an overall profit margin of 58.6%.
  • Across the sales period, the business recorded approximately 26K orders, sold 198K units, served 12K unique customers, and achieved an average order value of $2.1K.
  • Revenue increased from $6.9M in 2016 to a peak of $18.3M in 2019, and then declined to $9.3M in 2020 and $1.0M in 2021.

Product Performance

  • Adventure Works was the highest-revenue brand, generating approximately $12M in revenue and $7M in profit, followed by Contoso with $11M in revenue.
  • Computers was the leading product category, generating approximately $19M in revenue and $11M in profit, substantially ahead of Home Appliances at $11M in revenue.
  • Desktops was the highest-revenue sub-category, generating $9.9M in revenue and $5.6M in profit. Televisions ranked second with $4.3M in revenue.
  • The top individual product by revenue was WWI Desktop PC2.33 X2330 Black, generating approximately $0.51M in revenue and $0.34M in profit.

Customer & Store Performance

  • Customers aged 56+ generated the highest revenue at approximately $23M, followed by the 36–55 age group at $17M. The 13–19 age group contributed the least, at approximately $3M.
  • Revenue contribution by gender was nearly balanced, with male customers contributing $28.3M (50.82%) and female customers contributing $27.4M (49.18%).
  • Physical stores generated approximately $44M in revenue and $26M in profit, compared with $11M in revenue and $7M in profit from online sales.

Geographic Performance

  • The United States was the largest market by a substantial margin, generating approximately $24M in revenue, followed by the United Kingdom at $6M.
  • Among states shown in the top-five ranking, Nevada generated the highest revenue at approximately $1.42M, closely followed by Kansas ($1.39M) and Nebraska ($1.38M).
